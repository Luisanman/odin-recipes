Cold Tofu Bowl

A quick, refreshing, protein-packed meal perfect for vegan weightlifters.

This cold tofu bowl can be used as a base for a wide variety of dishes. In this recipe, we will be focusing on a teriyaki and sesame seed dish.

Ingredients

  • Super Firm Tofu
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Paprika
  • Sea Salt
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Press tofu until water is completely drained when flipped upside down.
  2. Chop tofu into cubes of your desired size.
  3. Add Paprika and salt to taste.
  4. Pour the Teriyaki sauce to cover all of your tofu.
  5. Garnish with Sesame Seeds and Seaweed
