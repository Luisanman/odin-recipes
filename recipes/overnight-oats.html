Overnight Oats

The perfect quick meal to power the first half of your day.

Using this recipe as a template, you can eat this every morning with enough variety to spice things up.

Ingredients

  • Whole Oats
  • High Protein Soy Milk
  • Berries
  • Low Fat Peanut Butter
  • Granola
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Press tofu until water is completely drained when flipped upside down.
  2. Chop tofu into cubes of your desired size.
  3. Add Paprika and salt to taste.
  4. Pour the Teriyaki sauce to cover all of your tofu.
  5. Garnish with Sesame Seeds and Seaweed
