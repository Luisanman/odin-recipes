Overnight Oats

The perfect quick meal to power the first half of your day.

Using this recipe as a template, you can eat this every morning with enough variety to spice things up.

Ingredients

  • Whole Oats
  • High Protein Soy Milk
  • Berries
  • Low Fat Peanut Butter
  • Granola
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Measure out desired milk in a mason jar.
  2. Add in Whole Oats.
  3. Add Berries.
  4. Add Low Fat Peanut Butter and stir.
  5. Add Granola and Nuts.
  6. Seal jar tightly and shake until contents are mixed.
  7. Store in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
