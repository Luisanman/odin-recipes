Tofu Stir Fry

Easy, nutritous, stir-fry for vegan weightlifters.

Using this recipe as a template, you can eat this every lunch with enough variety to spice things up.

Ingredients

  • High Protein Tofu
  • Mixed Veggies
  • Olive Oil
  • Seaweed
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Press and chop tofu into desired size.
  2. Add oil and veggies to wok on high heat.
  3. Stir soy sauce and sesame seeds in.
  4. Add tofu and mix in.
  5. Garnish with Seaweed.
